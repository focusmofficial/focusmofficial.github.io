Whitepaper

Focus and earn. It's simple!

Focusm is a Web3 mobile application with Game-Fi and Social-Fi elements.

Idea

The world is beautiful, but we spend a lot of time on the phone. We absorb various content, forgetting about the most important thing. We don't have time for live communication, sports training and self-development.

Focusm will help you concentrate on the task, thereby saving you time.

App
How does it work?
Step 1

Download the Focusm application on the phone.

Step 2

Buys any plant in the form of NFT on the marketplace.

Step 3

Sets a timer for a specific time that he needs to focus on.

Step 4

Selects the type of activity that user performs.

Step 5

Presses Start.

Step 6

User earns an game token GROW for focusing.

Token $FCS & $GROW

$FCS are main or external governance tokens. They benefit from ecosystem charges. $GROW are internal application tokens (in-app token).

$GROW tokens are earned for focusing while staking (focusing) NFTs (PLANTs). $GROW tokens can be exchanged for $FCS. $FCS tokens allow you to buy NFTs (PLANTs) on the secondary market (NFT Marketplace).
